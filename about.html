ABOUT ME

I started out as a Geoscientist but made the transition to tech about a year ago. To expand my skill set, I am studying data analytics with Python on DataCamp. This is because my knowledge on Earth’s systems and analytical skills can be a valuable asset in the tech industry. Currently I work as a Training Assistant at Soronko Academy helping to train over 100 young ladies with digital skills under the Rotary Girls’ Coding and the MasterCard Women in Digital Skills projects. Additionally, on the weekends, I mentor a group of young women from Nigeria and Kenya studying data analytics with Eloquent Data, a Pan-African data community, by supporting them in their data learning journey.
